EDUGO ™ is a Unique Education Management System that aims to enhance the learning experience through improving the experience and engagement of all stakeholders involved in the learning Process.
EDUGO ™ started as an idea in 2013 with a vision to become a leader in the field of Education & Learning management system. With a small and ambitious team we started building our dream and introducing its concept and early versions to the market.
Over the past 2 years we succeeded in building the first version of EDUGO ™ and introducing it to 2 Educational institutes in Egypt Only.
We have also introduced EDUGO version2 now available with enhanced user experience and enhanced features.
